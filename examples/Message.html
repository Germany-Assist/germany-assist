Messages Guide

Stay in direct contact with your clients. Fast, clear communication leads to better reviews and fewer disputes.

How messaging works
💬
Order-linked chats. Each order has its own conversation thread — keeping all order-related communication in one place.
📎
Share files. Attach documents, images, or notes directly in the chat to avoid back-and-forth emails.
🔔
Notifications. You'll be notified whenever a client sends a new message — respond promptly to keep your response rate high.
Pro tips
⚡
Reply within 24h. A high response rate boosts your visibility in search results and builds client trust.
🛡️
Keep it on-platform. All messages are recorded — this protects you in case of a dispute.
👋
Welcome new clients. Send a quick intro message when you accept an order to set expectations early.
