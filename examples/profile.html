My Profile Guide

Your profile is what clients see before they order. A complete, professional profile builds trust and converts visitors into paying clients.

What to fill in
📸
Profile photo. Use a clear, professional headshot. Providers with photos receive significantly more orders.
✍️
Bio. Write 2–3 sentences about your expertise, background, and why clients should choose you. Keep it specific.
🗣️
Languages. List all languages you can work in — this expands your potential client base across Germany.
🎓
Qualifications. Add relevant certifications or education — clients value verified expertise especially for legal and financial services.
Pro tips
⭐
Complete = more visible. Fully completed profiles rank higher in search. Fill every field you can.
🔄
Keep it fresh. Update your bio and specialities as your skills grow — stale profiles lose rankings over time.
