How to update, pause, or deactivate a listing
Beginner3 min
1

Order Lifecycle: From Request to Completion
Understand every status and what action to take
Important10 min
1
New Request — Review within 24 hours
When a client books your service, you'll get a notification. Review the order details carefully. You can Accept or Decline within 24 hours. Declining too often lowers your ranking.
⚠ Providers who accept less than 70% of requests may have reduced visibility in search results.
2
In Progress — Communicate clearly
Once accepted, use the in-app chat to communicate. Request any missing documents early. Send progress updates at each milestone.
💡 Clients who receive at least one update within the first 12 hours are 3x less likely to cancel.
3
Delivery — Submit completed work
When ready, click Deliver Order and upload any files or documents. Write a clear summary of what was done for the client.
4
Revision — Handle feedback professionally
Clients can request 1 revision (included by default). Respond within 48 hours. If the request is out of scope, politely explain and offer a separate quote.
5
Completed — Ask for a review
Once the client confirms completion, the funds are released to your balance. The system sends an automatic review prompt, but you can send a personal thank-you message too.
💡 Providers with 10+ reviews earn a "Trusted Provider" badge, boosting click-through rates by 40%.

Balance, withdrawal schedule & bank setup
Beginner6 min

Understanding your balance

Verification Centre

Document Submission Guide
What to submit, accepted formats & review timeline
Required7 min
1
Identity verification (ID / Passport)
Upload a clear photo or scan of your valid passport or German ID card (Personalausweis). Both sides required for ID cards. File size: max 10MB, formats: PDF, JPG, PNG.
💡 Ensure all 4 corners are visible and text is readable. Blurry photos are the #1 reason for rejection.
2
Professional credentials (if applicable)
For legal, medical, tax, or licensed services: upload your professional certificate or license. For translators: upload language certification. All documents must be current.
3
Address proof
Upload a utility bill, bank statement, or Anmeldung confirmation showing your current German address. Must be dated within the last 3 months.
4
Review timeline
Standard review: 3–5 business days. You'll receive an email and in-app notification with the result. If rejected, you'll see the reason and can resubmit immediately.
⚠ Do not submit documents with personal information hidden or redacted — this causes automatic rejection.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long does verification take?+
Standard verification takes 3–5 business days. You'll receive an email and in-app notification. During busy periods (e.g. end of month) it may take up to 7 days. Make sure all documents are clear and fully visible to avoid delays.
What is the platform commission rate?+
Germany Assists charges a 15% service fee on each successfully completed order. This is automatically deducted before funds appear in your available balance. There are no monthly fees.
Can I have multiple service listings?+
Yes. You can create up to 20 active service listings simultaneously. We recommend creating separate listings for each distinct service you offer, as each one gets its own search ranking and reviews.
When do I receive my payout?+
After order completion and the 14-day clearance period, funds move to your Available balance. You can then request a manual payout or enable automatic weekly payouts. Bank transfers arrive within 3–5 business days.
What happens if a client doesn't respond after delivery?+
If the client doesn't respond within 5 days of delivery, the order is automatically marked as complete and funds are released. You'll receive a notification 2 days before auto-completion so you can follow up.
Can I offer services outside Germany?+
Germany Assists focuses on services for people in Germany, but many services can be delivered remotely to clients internationally. When creating your listing, you can specify whether the service is in-person, remote, or both.
My verification was rejected. What now?+
Check the rejection reason in the Verification Centre — it's shown next to the rejected document. Fix the issue (e.g., resubmit a clearer photo) and resubmit. There's no limit on resubmission attempts. Common reasons: blurry images, expired documents, missing corners.
