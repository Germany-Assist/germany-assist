Orders Guide

Here's how orders work on Germany Assists — from the moment a client places an order to when you receive your payment.

How an order works
1
Client Places an Order
A client finds your service, pays upfront, and submits the order. Payment goes into Escrow — secure until the order is complete.
⏳ Status: Pending
2
You Accept or Decline
You have 48 hours to review and accept the order. If you decline, the client is fully refunded. Once accepted, a deadline counter starts.
▶ Status: Active
3
Deliver Your Work
Complete the service before the deadline. Use the Deliver button to submit your work and a message to the client. Upload files if needed.
📦 Status: Delivered
4
Client Reviews (14 days)
The client has 14 days to approve or raise a dispute. If no action is taken, the order auto-completes and payment releases.
5
Payment Released
Once complete, your earnings are added to your GA Wallet balance. You can withdraw at any time from the Finance page.
✅ Status: Completed
Order status reference
Pending
Awaiting your acceptance. Review and accept within 48h or it expires.
Active
In progress. Deadline counter is running. Deliver before the due date.
Late
Past deadline. Act now — repeated lateness affects your ranking.
Delivered
Awaiting client approval. 14-day auto-release if no response.
Disputed
Under admin review. Respond with evidence via the order chat.
Completed
Payment released. Earnings are in your GA Wallet.
Pro tips
💬
Communicate early. Send a welcome message as soon as you accept — clients who hear from providers quickly leave better reviews.
📎
Attach documents. Upload checklists, templates, or notes directly in the order chat to keep everything in one place.
⚡
Deliver early. Orders completed ahead of schedule boost your provider score and unlock higher visibility in search.
🛡️
Disputes are rare. Clear communication and documented delivery reduce dispute risk to near zero.
