Settings Guide

Configure your account preferences, notification settings, payment details, and security options all in one place.

Key settings areas
🔔
Notifications. Choose how and when you receive alerts — email, in-app, or both — for new orders, messages, and payments.
💳
Payment Details. Add or update your bank account information for withdrawals. Always keep this up to date.
🔒
Security. Change your password and enable two-factor authentication to protect your account.
🌍
Language & Region. Set your preferred language and timezone for accurate deadline and scheduling displays.
Pro tips
✅
Enable all order notifications. Missing a new order notification means delayed acceptance — which can expire the order.
🛡️
Use a strong password. Your account holds financial data — a secure password is essential.
