Finance Guide

Manage your earnings, request withdrawals, and track every transaction from your GA Wallet.

How payments work
1
Order Completes
When an order is marked complete (by the client or auto-released after 14 days), earnings are added to your GA Wallet balance.
2
Request a Withdrawal
Click Withdraw and enter your bank details. Minimum withdrawal is €20. Funds are transferred within 3–5 business days.
3
Money in Your Bank
You'll receive a confirmation email once the transfer is processed. All transactions appear in your history below.
Pro tips
💳
Keep bank details updated. Outdated details delay withdrawals — check them in Settings.
📄
Download statements. Use the download button for monthly statements useful for tax records.
🔒
Escrow protects you. Client payments are held securely until you deliver — you always get paid for completed work.
