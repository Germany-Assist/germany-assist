Dashboard Guide

Your dashboard gives you a live snapshot of your business — earnings, orders, and performance all in one place.

What you can see
📊
KPI Cards. Track your total earnings, active orders, completed jobs, and average rating at a glance.

Period Switcher. Toggle between This Month, Last Month, and Custom to compare performance over time.

Earnings Chart. Visual breakdown of revenue trends. Spot slow weeks and plan accordingly.

Recent Activity. Latest order updates and client actions so you never miss an important event.
