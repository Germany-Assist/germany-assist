Notifications Guide

Stay on top of every order update, client message, and payment event. Notifications keep you informed without needing to check every page manually.

Notification types
📦
Order alerts. New orders, acceptance deadlines, delivery confirmations, and dispute updates.
💬
Message alerts. New client messages so you never miss a conversation.
💰
Payment alerts. Withdrawal confirmations and earnings releases to your GA Wallet.
🔔
System alerts. Platform announcements, policy updates, and account notices.
Pro tips
⚡
Don't ignore order alerts. New orders expire in 48h if not accepted — enable push or email notifications so you never miss one.
🎛️
Customise in Settings. Go to Settings to control which notifications you receive and how.
